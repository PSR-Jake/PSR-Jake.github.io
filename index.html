Welcome

Welcome to my personal website! I am Jiaqi Zhao (aka Jake; he/him), currently a PhD candidate in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of Alberta, working with Professor Craig Heinke. I was born and raised in Xinxiang City, Henan Province, China.

I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in 2018 from Southwest University in Chongqing, China. After taking a gap year, I began my Master’s program at the University of Alberta, where I continued my research under Professor Heinke. I completed my Master of Science degree in 2021 and have since been pursuing my doctoral degree.

My research focuses on X-ray observations and analysis of neutron stars, pulsars, in particular millisecond pulsars in globular clusters, etc. Feel free to explore my research interests, publications, and more.

If you'd like to get in touch, you can email me at jzhao11-at-ualberta.ca or connect with me on LinkedIn.
