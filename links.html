Resources I return to often

This page collects catalogs, archives, tools, and job resources that are especially useful in day-to-day compact-object and X-ray astronomy work.

Pulsar Catalogs

  • ATNF Pulsar Catalog for published rotation-powered pulsars, AXPs, and SGRs with coherent pulsations.
  • Pulsars in Globular Clusters for an up-to-date cluster pulsar compilation.
  • Galactic Millisecond Pulsars for field MSPs in the Milky Way.

Globular Cluster Catalogs

  • Catalog of Parameters for Milky Way Globular Clusters for classic distances, metallicities, luminosities, and dynamical parameters.
  • Fundamental Parameters of Galactic Globular Clusters for model-based cluster properties using Gaia DR3 and other inputs.

Data Archives

  • Chandra Data Archive and ChaSeR for Chandra data access.
  • XMM-Newton Science Archive for XMM mission data and related catalog searches.
  • MAST for optical, ultraviolet, and near-infrared mission archives including Webb and Hubble.
  • CDS and VizieR for widely used astronomical data services.

Tools

  • CIAO, Sherpa, and ds9 for X-ray reduction, fitting, and visualization.
  • BXA and UltraNest for Bayesian X-ray analysis workflows.
  • Chandra Proposal Planning Toolkit for practical conversions and observation planning.
  • photutils and TOPCAT for photometry and tabular data exploration.

Article Search & Collection

  • NASA ADS for literature search, citation tracking, and alerts.
  • arXiv for recent astro-ph submissions.

Academic Jobs

  • AAS Job Register
  • Academic Jobs Online
  • EAS Job Directory
